Most failed technology leadership appointments are not caused by hiring the wrong person. They are caused by an incomplete brief, one that misrepresents the mandate, overstates the conditions for success, and leaves the incoming leader to discover the gap after they have started. This diagnostic is designed to surface that gap before the search begins.

We are asking you to contribute to two connected areas of thinking. The first is a structured interview that explores the strategic and organisational context this appointment sits in; your view of where things stand today and where they need to be. The second is a competency profile, drawn from Agency Three's EQ/IQ leadership framework, that defines the specific behaviours this role demands.

When all four stakeholder groups have completed their input, the diagnostic will generate a world-class role description that accurately reflects the opportunity, the real challenges, and the leadership profile required to be successful in it. This is the brief behind the brief; it is the most important document in the search.

Agency Three EQ/IQ Framework
The leadership assessment framework behind this diagnostic

Agency Three's EQ/IQ framework is a proprietary leadership assessment instrument developed over 25 years of senior technology executive search and assessment. It evaluates leaders across two interconnected dimensions: IQ (the intellectual and technical capability required to operate at this level) and EQ (the emotional and interpersonal capability required to lead effectively in complex, senior environments).

The framework comprises 10 competency groups, each containing three specific leadership behaviours. It has been used across more than 500 senior technology leadership placements and 7,000 interviews to build a highly reliable picture of what genuinely differentiates effective technology leaders from plausible ones.

Rating Scale
Competent

Strong

Exceptional
